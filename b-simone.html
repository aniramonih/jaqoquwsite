B. Simone (born April 5, 1990) is an American Actress, Singer, Songwriter, Entrepreneur, and Instagram Star from Dallas, Texas. She first garnered popularity after starring in the MTV show called “Wild ‘N Out” in 2017.

Furthermore, the singer made her debut in the music industry with an Extended Play, Lost Soul in 2014. Her recent songs are “Raw Shit Freestyle”, “The Box”, “Million Dollar Freestyle”, and “Wet Jewels”.

Simone also released singles such as “Her”, “In My Bag”, “Blueberry Rain”, “Intro”, “London Bae”, “Read Receipts”, “Just Another Day”, “Mixed Feelings”, “Trap Wife”, “How Sway”, “XL Mad”, etc.
