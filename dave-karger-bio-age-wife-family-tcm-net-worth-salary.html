Dave Karger Biography

Dave Karger is a renowned American award-winning host, interviewer, and entertainment expert currently hosting Turner Classic Movies (TCM). Karger began guest hosting on TCM in 2016 and was named an official host in 2018. Currently, he has made more than 200 live appearances on NBC’s Today Show and also serves as a Special Correspondent E! and Access Hollywood. In 2018, Dave co-hosted ABC’s Live from the Red Carpet on Oscar night.

Dave earned his bachelor’s degree from Duke University. In 2015, he received the Publicists Guild Press Award, which recognizes the year’s best entertainment journalist. He was named one of OUT Magazine’s “OUT 100,” in 2014, recognizing the most influential people in the LGBT community. Dave has also appeared on Today, Access Hollywood Live, and Turner Classic Movies as a guest host.

He was the Chief Correspondent at Fandango from 2012 to 2016, where he hosted the original video series “The Frontrunners” and “Weekend Ticket.” “The Frontrunners” was nominated for a Webby Award for Best Variety Series in 2013.

Dave previously worked as a senior writer at Entertainment Weekly for 17 years, where he wrote over 50 cover stories on celebrities such as George Clooney, Angelina Jolie, Elton John, Taylor Swift, Johnny Depp, Denzel Washington, and Carrie Underwood. He also oversaw the magazine’s and EW.com’s Oscar coverage.

Dave was only the third person to serve as the Academy’s official red-carpet greeter on Oscar night in 2012 and 2013. He also co-hosted The Academy’s and ABC’s 2011 Oscars Digital Experience, which won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Creative Achievement in Interactive Media. He has served on the juries of the TriBeCa, Los Angeles, and South By Southwest, and Florida Film Festivals.
