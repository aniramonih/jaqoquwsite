Does David Beckham Have Brother? Meet Sister Joanne And Lynne

Publish date: 2024-08-22

The former professional soccer player David Beckham, does not have a brother. He has two sisters, Lynne Georgina Beckham and Joanne Louise Beckham.

David is very close to his two siblings. They are more like friends than brothers and sisters.

Also, the former soccer player frequently appreciates his sisters’s support and love towards him.

Despite a considerable fame and career, David has always been a caring brother to his two sisters. 

Speaking of which, David is regarded as one of the most prominent soccer players that the world has ever seen.

He began his journey to become the most outstanding player after debuting his youth career in 1987 with Tottenham Hotspur. 

Likewise, in 1992, David began his senior year. He played 523 games for clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, LA Galaxy, Paris Saint-Germain, and more.

Also, David has a record of playing 115 games for England from 1996 to 2009.

After bidding farewell to soccer in 2013, he has been indulged in various business sectors.

He is the co-owner of Inter Miami and Salford City.

In the meantime, David has accumulated a net worth of $450 million, making him one of the wealthiest athletes.

Meet David Beckham’s Older Sister, Lynne Georgina Beckham

Lynne was born on May 29, 1972, in London, England. She is 51 years old.

In 1999, Lynne exchanged the wedding vows with her estranged now husband, Colin Every. Their family and close friends attended the wedding.

Also, David showed his appearance at Lynne’s wedding by wearing a black tuxedo.

After a few years, Lynne and Colin gave birth to three children named Georgina, Freddie, and Josh.

However, the marriage between Lyne and Colin couldn’t handle the rough times and differences for a long time.

As a result, they divorced each other in the end. Also, there was a report that David was not happy about the marriage in the first place.

Currently, Lynne is living a happy life around her well-wishers and children. Being a private person, she has kept her personal life away from the limelight and media.

Insight Into Joanne Louise Beckham

David’s younger sister, Joanne, was born in London, England. 

Joanne was in a serious relationship with her ex-partner, the Big Brother contestant Kris Donnelly, for a long time.

The former couple began seeing each other in 2009. They were so in love with each other.

Everything was going well for many years; however, things took different turns as the days passed. As a result, Joanne and Kris ended their relationship after being together for nearly eight years.

Also, just before moving a different path, the former duo gave birth to their daughter, Peggy, in December 2017.

As they share a child, Joanne and Kris are still good friends.

Info On Her Profession

Joanne was inspired by her mother, Sandra Beckham, a lot. So, she chose to make her career as a hairdresser.

As a result, she joined a training session for the hairdresser at Vidal Sassoon Salon in London.

After that, Joanne worked for the Essex.

Also, she established the concierge service called WeAreYourCity, along with Jay Smith, the former professional soccer player.

Within a time frame, that business began to flourish and had over 10,400 followers on its Instagram handle.

However, the page has been inactive since 2021.

Brief Info On David Beckham’s Married Life

The former soccer player, David, has been married to his wife, Victoria Beckham, since 1999.

Victoria is a former singer of the girl group Spice Girls. Now, she serves as a fashion designer and a television personality.

David and Victoria are parents to their four children, including Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz, and Harper.
