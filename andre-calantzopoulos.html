Who Actually is Andre Calantzopoulos?

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Andre Calantzopoulos is a familiar name when it comes to the corporate world. He is the former CEO and present executive chairman of Philip Morris International (PMI). 

Calantzopoulos was born in the year 1957 in Pyrgos, Greece. He has been in the world of business for more than three decades.

What is PMI?

Philip Morris International Inc. (PMI) is a cigarette company that also manufactures tobacco. It is a Swiss-American multi-national corporation that has subsidiaries in more than 180 nations across the globe.

The company aims to produce smoke-free cigarettes making cigarette smoking environment as well as health-friendly.

Who is Calantzopoulos?

Andre is a Greek national with expertise in business strategy and development, sales, public speaking, and start-ups. 

The mailing address of Calantzopoulos is 120, Park Avenue, City of Cohoes, Albany County, New York, 12047, United States.

Career Started From Automobile

At the beginning of his career, Andre worked in the automobile industry. He served as a design engineer at that time.

In the year 1985, Calantzopoulos joined PMI. For several years, he worked in different regions of Europe. He was once a Managing Director of PM in Poland. Further, he even worked as the President of Eastern Europe.

Net Worth of Calantzopoulos

Andre Calantzopoulos is a rich businessman. He has a salary of $22 million as the Director at PMI. As of 2022, Andre has a total net worth of $165 million. Further, he has more than forty thousand stock units of Philip Morris International Inc. 

Moreover, Calantzopouos and his wife bought a 316ha farm in the Bay of Islands in July 2017.
