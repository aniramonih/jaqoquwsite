50 Cent mocks Madonna by likening her to an alien over her recent raunchy photo

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Madonna’s recent raunchy photo has prompted a mocking response from 50 Cent, who likened her to an alien. The singer shared a suggestive picture of herself on Instagram, wearing cat-eye sunglasses while sitting on a bed with her legs spread open. In response, 50 Cent re-posted the photo and humorously commented about her age, suggesting that someone should tell her to “chill out” at 63. Additionally, he shared three photos of aliens alongside Madonna’s picture, implying that she resembles an extraterrestrial in her photo. He also questioned if Madonna had her children take the picture. The mocking post by 50 Cent highlighted the singer’s bold Instagram image.
