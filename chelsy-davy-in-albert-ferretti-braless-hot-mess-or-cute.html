Chelsy Davy in Alberta Ferretti: braless hot mess or cute?

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Oh. Dear. God. Chelsy Davy is presumed to be “next” as far royal weddings go, and this is how she chooses to represent herself to her possible future sister- and brother-in-law? As we heard before, Chelsy is being dressed exclusively (in two outfits) by Alberti Ferretti. All I can say is that the girl must not have shown up for any fittings, because the fit on this suit is a mess. As is her hair. As is the whole situation.

I do like the color on her, though. Somebody had the bright idea to put her in seafoam green, which is a good instinct. Unfortunately, she must have been up until 3 am, getting hammered with the boys. That’s what her hair looks like.

Oh, and she’s not wearing a bra, is she?
