What is Pia Guanio's Net Worth?

Pia Guanio is a Filipino television presenter who has a net worth of $4 million dollars. Pia Guanio was born Liana Pia Coronado Guanio on October 24, 1973 in Manila, Philippines. She attended the University of the Philippines where she obtained her degree in Mass Communications with an emphasis on Broadcast. She is head anchor for the entertainment program "Showbiz Central". She is also the showbiz segment anchor for the GMA Network primetime newscast "24 Oras" as well as co-host alongside her former boyfriend Vic Sotto on the noontime talk show "Eat Bulaga". After the couple broke up, she remained with the show.

She began her career in entertainment as a lively traffic reporter for CALTEX's "RoadWatch" in 1999. Guanio caught the attention of travelogue channel Lakbay TV and her career has been on the move ever since. After Lakbay TV, Guanio was one of the hosts of ABS-CBN's morning show "Alas Singko Y Medya". Her biggest career move though was when she transferred from the Kapamilya network to the GMA Network. Guanio married businessman Steve Mago in October 2011. She give birth to her first child, Scarlet Jenine, on August 2012 and went on a three month hiatus on "24 Oras".
