Presently, Christian works as a senior biomedical editor at Health Science Communications in New York City. Christian Cooper’s Relationship Status

Christian Cooper is not married; He publicly acknowledged his homosexuality during his freshman year at Harvard in 1981. Cooper broke new ground as Marvel’s first gay writer and editor, introducing Yoshi Mishima as the first gay male character in the Star Trek franchise’s Starfleet Academy series. His contribution to Star Trek earned a GLAAD Media Award nomination in 1999.

Cooper’s pioneering efforts extended to Marvel, where he introduced Victoria Montesi as the company’s first openly lesbian character and founded and authored Queer Nation: The Online Gay Comic. He also worked as an assistant editor for Marvel Comics and wrote stories for Marvel Comics Presents. He also edited various X-Men collections and completed the last two issues of the Marvel Swimsuit Special.
