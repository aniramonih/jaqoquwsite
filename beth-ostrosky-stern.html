Beth Ostrosky Stern News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Name:Beth Ostrosky Stern

Hometown:Pittsburgh, PA

Birthday:July 15, 1972

About

This blonde ex-model, actress and TV personality (she hosted ABC’s True Beauty) married syndicated radio shock jock Howard Stern in 2008. Named one of FHM‘s “Top 100 Sexiest Women of the Year” in 2002, 2003, 2004, and 2007, Beth Ostrosky Stern is an avid animal lover, who has helped The Wildlife Rescue Center of the Hamptons and the North Shore Animal League America.

She is also the author of Oh My Dog: How to Raise Your Pet to Be a Happy, Disciplined, Healthy, Fun, and Friendly Ball of Love, which was released in 2010.
