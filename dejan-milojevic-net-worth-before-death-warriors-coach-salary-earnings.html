Dejan Milojevic (born on April 15, 1977) was a famous professional basketball player and coach who served as an assistant coach for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA) at the time of his death on January 17, 2024.

Milojević played professionally as a power forward from 1994 until 2009 and appeared for the FR Yugoslavia / Serbia and Montenegro league teams Beovuk, FMP, Budućnost, and Partizan.

The 6-foot-7-inch sportsman played for Pamesa Valencia in Spain and Galatasaray in Turkey. Also, Dejan was named the Adriatic League Most Valuable Player back-to-back thrice in 2004, 2005, and 2006.

Talking about his coaching career, he remained active as a coach from 2012 to 2024. Likewise, Milojević was the assistant coach for the Serbia national team under Igor Kokoškov, which he left in September 2021.
