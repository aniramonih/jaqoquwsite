Who is Awra Briguela?

Awra Briguela is a Filipino child actor plus comedian. He is popular for his role in “The Super Parental Guardians” (2016) and “Maalaala mo kaya” (1991).

Awra Briguela: Birth, Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

He was born to Asian parents on March 26, 2004, in Las Piñas, Philippines. He holds Asian ethnicity.

Details regarding his father, mother, and siblings’ are not disclosed yet.

Nothing is published about his educational background.

Awra Briguela: Professional Life, Career

Awra Briguela made his professional acting debut in the year 2016 and appeared in a film titled “The Super Parental Guardians” as ‘Melvin Gaspar’.

In the same year, he made his T.V debut with “Ang Probinsyano” as ‘Macario Samonte, Jr.’

He is still associated with the show since then and is gaining huge popularity. He appeared as himself in a T.V show “Maalaala Mo Kaya: Awra” in 2016.

In the year 2017, Awra Briguela appeared in a total of 2 T.V shows titled “Pusong Ligaw” as ‘young Asiong’ and “Your Face Sounds Familiar: Kids” as ‘Contestant’ Later in, he became the grand winner of the show.

Also, Awra Briguela is the winner of the “30th PMPC Star Awards for TV” and “3rd Aral Parangal Awards” for a movie titled “FPJ’s Ang Probinsyano” in the category of ‘Best Child Performer’.
