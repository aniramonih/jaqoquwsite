Who is Ethan Hutchison?

Ethan Hutchison is a talented American child actor. He rose to fame after portraying the role of Blue Bordelon in the television series titled “Queen Sugar.”

He also has appeared as Jake Gaines in the Hulu Original TV series titled, “The Path” (2016).

Ethan Hutchison- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Ethan Hutchison was born in Long Island, New York, United States on September 7, 2010. Hutchison is 11 years old as of 2022 and holds American nationality. His ethnicity is mixed and as per the astrologers, his birth zodiac sign is Virgo.

His father, Eric Hutchison is a computer engineer and digital from New Orleans, and his mother, Farrah La Ronde is an environmental scientist and documentary Film-producer from the Caribbean island of Dominica. He is being raised with his younger sister named Ava. There are no details about his educational background.

Ethan Hutchison- Professional Life, Careers

Hutchison was passionate about acting since the age of three. After that, Ethan Hutchison appeared in the Hulu Original TV series, titled “The Path” in 2016 playing the role of Jake Gaines when he was just four years old after passing the audition.

Again, he got selected in his second audition which got him the chance to appear as Blue Bordelon on OWN’s original TV series titled, “Queen Sugar”.

Similarly, his appearance in “Queen Sugar”, earned him both the Young Entertainer Award and Young Artist Award in the category of Best Leading Young Actor.

Likewise, this series also got Ethan Hutchison the nomination for the NAACP Image Award for Outstanding  Performance by a Youth.

He was nominated for the People’s Choice Award for Favorite Cable TV Drama and received NAACP Image Award in the category of Outstanding Drama Series.
