Anthony Barr contract, salary and net worth explored

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Table of Content

Anthony Barr is a professional football player who plays primarily in the outside linebacker role.

Anthony Barr Contract

Barr signed a four-year, $12.7 million contract with the Vikings. He received a $7.6 million signing bonus and the team could exercise a fifth-year option.

Barr was rumored to be inking a multi-year contract with the New York Jets in March of 2019.  However, the outside linebacker re-signed with the Vikings on a five-year deal worth over $67 million of which $33 million was guaranteed.

Anthony Barr joined the Dallas Cowboys on a one-year deal worth $2 million with a $500,000 signing bonus. The deal also had a clause that ensured $1.75 million of his contract was guaranteed.

Anthony Barr Salary per year

He also receives a $2 million average yearly salary on his current deal. According to some sources, the salary is broken down into a $1.25 million basic salary, a $500,000 signing bonus, and a $250,000 roster bonus.

Anthony Barr Net worth

He has an estimated net worth of $50 million.
