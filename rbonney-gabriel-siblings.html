R’Bonney Gabriel was crowned Miss Universe 2022 by the outgoing titleholder Harnaaz Sandhu, making her the 9th American to win the pageant. She became the first American since Olivia Culpo of Miss Universe 2012 to win the title of Miss Universe thanks to her success.

R’Bonney Gabriel biography

Gabriel’s full name is R’Bonney Nola Gabriel. She was born in Houston, Texas, to an American mother Dana Walker and Filipino father Remigio Bonzon “R. Bon” Gabriel.

Her mom hails from Beaumont, Texas. Gabriel graduated from the University of North Texas with a bachelor’s degree in fashion design with a minor in fibers. She currently works as a model and a designer of sustainable apparel.
