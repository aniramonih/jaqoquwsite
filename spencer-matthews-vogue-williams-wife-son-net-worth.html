Who is Spencer Matthews and what is his net worth? The US Sun

Publish date: 2024-08-26

SPENCER Matthews found fame appearing on Made In Chelsea and his career has gone from strength to strength since.

He continues to grace our screens, but who is he and what is his net worth?

Who is Spencer Matthews?

Socialite Spencer Matthews, born on August 6, 1989, is the son of landowner David and successful artist Jane Matthews.

His brother James Matthews is married to Pippa Middleton and is the heir to a Scottish title, Laird of Glen Afraig, a glen located in the village of Cannich in the Highlands.

Tragically, Spencer’s oldest brother Michael died in a mountaineering accident in 1999 on Mount Everest, just hours after becoming the youngest Briton to conquer the peak.

Spencer is fronting Finding Michael, a documentary where he travels to Everest to try and find his brother's body.

Reality star Spencer was put through a tip-top education at Eton, and split his childhood between St Barths and Caunton Maon in Lincolnshire.

In 2014, he revealed that he is actually “northern at heart” because he was born in Sheffield.

In 2019, he revealed he was forced to hide in a vault inside a watch shop as it was smashed to pieces by thieves threatening staff with "huge hammers".

The same year, Spencer along with his wife Vogue Williams starred on their own E4 show, Spencer, Vogue And Baby Too.

The show was so popular with audiences that it returned for a second series the same year.

In 2018, Spencer featured on Celebrity MasterChef.

What's Spencer Matthew's net worth?

Spencer is thought to be worth around £12.5million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The star hasn’t just lived off his parents’ fortune though – he has made his own money since he started his career as a PR manager at posh London clubs, such as Movida.

He also embarked on a career as a trader and reportedly earned £100,000 a year doing that while filming Made In Chelsea at the same time.

Other earnings come from being a shareholder in his parent’s hotel, Eden Rock in the Caribbean.

In 2021, The Sun reported that Spencer's wealth had more than doubled in a year — from £21million to £47million

When did Spencer Matthews marry Vogue Williams?

In 2011, Spencer joined the cast of a structured reality TV show, Made In Chelsea, which follows the lives of affluent socialites from West London.

He remained on the show until 2016, delighting fans with his womanising ways and witty lines.

In 2012 he appeared as "the bachelor" in The Bachelor UK series 5, which saw him pick Khloe Evans to be his girlfriend.

The romance ended a week after the show ended.

Then in 2015 he won a coveted spot on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! but he was forced to leave after just three days, after he confessed to producers that he’d been taking Class C steroid medication in preparation for a charity boxing match that never went ahead, leading show staff to conclude it would be unsafe for him to stay.

Vogue and Spencer tied the knot in 2018.

The pair had their first child, son called Theodore in September of that same year.

In 2020 their daughter Gigi was born, with their second son Otto arriving in April 2022.

Who has Spencer Matthews dated?

Spencer started dating the Irish model Vogue Williams after meeting on the reality TV show The Jump.

The couple announced their engagement in February 2018 and got married in June 2018 at Spencer's family estate in Scotland.

Before settling down Spencer had a long romance with Caggie Dunlop, which was documented on Made In Chelsea's first three series.

He also had a fling with Louise Thompson which continued after he split from Caggie – until Louise found out he cheated on her.

Other MIC cast members he has dated include Lucy Watson, Stephanie Pratt, and Lauren Hutton.

Spencer briefly dated Geordie Shore’s Vicky Pattison and romanced model Morgane Robart.
