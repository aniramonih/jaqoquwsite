About Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel

American singer and actor Justin Timberlake began dating actress Jessica Biel in January 2007, after failed relationships with previous celebs Britney Spears, Emma Bunton and Cameron Diaz.

Timberlake and Biel sailed through the years together, got engaged in December 2011 and on October 19, 2012, they tied the knots in Italy.

Together the couple have two sons born in April 2015, and July 2020.

Timberlake remains one of the world’s best-selling music artists, with more than 88 million records sold.

The ten-time Grammy Award winner is also regarded as one of the best performing male soloist in history, beginning with his debut solo album, Justified in 2002, and several hit singles like “Cry Me a River” and “Rock Your Body”, “SexyBack”, “My Love” and “What Goes Around… Comes Around”, and “Give It to Me”.

As an actor, Timberlake appeared in the films The Love Guru (2008), The Social Network (2010), Bad Teacher (2011), Friends with Benefits (2011), and In Time (2011).

On the other hand, Jessica Biel is an award-winning actress who gained recognition for playing a lead role in the horror film The Texas Chainsaw Massacre in 2003.

Other films in which she has appeared include The Rules of Attraction (2002), Blade: Trinity (2004), Stealth (2005), The Illusionist (2006), I Now Pronounce You Chuck & Larry (2007), Valentine’s Day (2010), The A-Team (2010), New Year’s Eve (2011), Total Recall (2012), and Hitchcock (2012).
