David Esch: Who is Annika Sorenstam first husband?

Sorenstam and Esch met in 1994 on the driving range at Moon Valley Country Club, Phoenix, Arizona. At the 1995 Evian Masters, they were engaged. They got married in Lake Tahoe on January 4, 1997, and later got divorced in 2005.

After her divorce, she got engaged to Mike McGee in August 2007. McGee is the son of former PGA Tour and Champions Tour player Jerry McGee.

Later they got married on January 10, 2009, at Lake Nona Golf & Country Club in Orlando, Florida.

What caused their divorce?

Sorenstam filed for divorce after eight years of marriage. However, the details of the divorce are not available to the public.

Did they have children together?

They did not have any children together. However, she has two children with her current husband, Mike McGee; Ava Madelyn McGee, and William Nicholas McGee.
