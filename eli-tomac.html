Career

Eli Tomac is an American professional Motocross and Supercross racer. He is competing in the AMA Supercross and Motocross championships; a 2-Time Premier Class Supercross Champion and a 4-Time Outdoor Motocross Champion.

Tomac began his professional career in 2010, winning his first race in his rookie debut. Further, he made his 450cc debut part-way into the 2013 Supercross schedule and moved to the class full-time in 2014.

Moreover, he won his first outdoor 450cc event at the 2014 Spring Creek National in Millville, Minnesota, US. His first 450cc Supercross win came in 2015 at Chase Field in Phoenix, Arizona. He has been part of the Kawasaki factory racing team since the 2016 season. Then, he joined Yamaha for the 2022 season.

Furthermore, in 2022 Eli won a record-setting 6th Daytona Supercross passing previous record holder Ricky Carmichael with 5. At the moment, he is currently 3rd in All-Time Motocross Wins with 28. Likewise, he is currently tied with Chad Reed for 4th All-Time in 450 Supercross Wins with 44.
