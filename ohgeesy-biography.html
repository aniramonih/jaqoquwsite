Who is OHGEESY?

OHGEESY is an American rapper who is involved with a group named Shoreline Mafia. His music genre is Hip Hop/Rap.

His singles are “Da Move” and “Heavy”. He has a huge number of followers on Instagram and Twitter.

He was born on 27 May 1993 in Los Angeles, the USA with the real name Alejandro Coranza. But details about his father, mother, and siblings are not yet revealed.

Moreover, his ancestry is Caucasian whereas his academic history is unknown.

OHGEESY- Professional Life, Career

OHGEESY is one of the popular rappers. In 2012, an unofficial group Shoreline Mafia was formed In LA. Then in 2016, they became official. Together they have sung songs like “Musty”, “Bottle Service”, “Nun Major”, “Whass Da Deal”, “Spaceship”, and “Serve a Boat”.

The other members of this group are Fenix (former), Master Kato, and Rob Vicious. The EPs of this group are Party Pack (2018) and Party Pack, Vol. 2 (2019). Recently in July 2020, with Atlantic Records, Shoreline Mafia brought an album Mafia Bidness for the public.
