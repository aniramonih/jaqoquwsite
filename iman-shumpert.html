Iman Shumpert is an American professional basketball player who has represented the New York Knicks (2011-2015), the Cleveland Cavaliers (2015-2018), the Sacramento Kings (2018-2019), the Houston Rockets (2019), and the Brooklyn Nets (2019-2021), as a shooting guard, in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He was a member of the Cleveland Cavaliers squad that had won the NBA Championship in 2016. He was drafted 17th overall in the 1st round of the 2011 NBA Draft by the New York Knicks.

Education

Iman had attended the Gwendolyn Brooks Middle School in Oak Park, Illinois. He had later studied at the Oak Park and River Forest High School, a public school in Oak Park, Illinois.

After his high school graduation in 2008, he had joined the Georgia Institute of Technology, a public research university, and institute of technology in Atlanta, Georgia. He had represented the Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets men’s basketball team there until March 2011 when he had declared for the NBA Draft, forgoing his final year of college eligibility.
