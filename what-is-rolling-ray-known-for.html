What Is Rolling Ray Known For?

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Internet personality and musician, Rolling Ray, is famous for his appearance on ‘MTV Catfish: Trolls’.

He once came on Instagram Live to reveal an accident that has left his ‘legs, arms, feet’ burned.

The infamous social media sensation was hospitalized after he was hit by a car while crossing the street.

A tweet including a picture of him laying in a hospital bed with his motorized wheelchair nearby, was posted.

“Pray for RollingRay guys,” the tweet read. “A car hit him while he was crossing the street.”

Ray became an Internet sensation through his appearance on MTV’s “Catfish: Trolls” which captured his dispute with another viral star named “Camyonce.”

Ray, who had been wheelchair-bound at the time, was involved in an accident in 2018 when a car hit him while he was crossing the street

He first took to Instagram in 2018 and since then, has accumulated over 350,000 followers.

The rapper started releasing music in 2018, but it looks as though Rolling Ray’s life dramas are gaining more attention than his music in 2021.
