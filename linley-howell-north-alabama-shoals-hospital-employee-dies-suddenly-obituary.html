As the family grieves this unimaginable loss, the entire community is urged to keep them in their thoughts and prayers during this difficult time of mourning and remembrance.

Linley Howell, a dedicated worker at North Alabama Shoals Hospital, had her roots firmly planted in Killen, Alabama, where she spent her early years before relocating to Muscle Shoals, Alabama, where she resided until her untimely passing.
