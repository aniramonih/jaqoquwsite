The Ultimatum: Marry Or Move On: Are Roxanne And Antonio Still Together? Relationship

Antonio gave Roxanne an ultimatum. For the past four years, the pair have been dating intermittently.

Antonio knew Roxanne was wife material the instant he laid eyes on her. She also felt a strong connection, but the two would only meet for a while.

When they reunited on social media, it was evident to both that they were meant to be. They’ve been dating for four years, on and off, and life has only improved.

Marriage is the obvious next step in their relationship for, Antonio, but it is the last thing on his career-focused mind.

Roxanne’s thoughts are on a wedding. She first wants her life mate to be financially and professionally compatible with her, and Antonio has fallen short of her expectations.

While he admires her entrepreneurial spirit, Antonio wishes she would pay more attention to him, especially when expressing her emotions.

Antonio is now issuing the request to Roxanne because he believes “that kind of pressure needs to be applied” for her to contemplate accepting a proposal genuinely.

Roxanne And Antonio’s Relationship

Antonio is ready to marry, but Roxanne is focused on her job and is still determining if she wants to marry someone who doesn’t share her desire and wealth.

This pair in The Ultimatum: Marry or Move On had yet to learn they were intended to be together.

While Antonio craves marriage, Roxanne desires that her mate share her aspirations and income.

Antonio wishes for more affection, despite admiring her commercial ambition. As a result, the request is issued.

As he reflected on his feelings for her, Antonio discovered a strange juxtaposition of emotions. He couldn’t help but appreciate her unshakable determination and dedication to her commercial goals.

Roxanne’s passion and ambition were inspirational, propelling her to success in a world of fierce competition.

Nonetheless, subtle anguish crept in his heart despite his admiration – a need for more tenderness, for moments that transcended the constraints of the professional arena.

Antonio craved wordless glances and delicate gestures that indicated a bond deeper than business companionship.

How does ‘The Ultimatum’ work?

At the show’s outset, all the couples and co-hosts, Nick and Vanessa Lachey greet. They go on dates for a few days before choosing a partner for a three-week trial marriage.

The new couples live together and become acquainted with each other’s friends and family. After three weeks, the candidates return to their original partners and move in for another three-week trial marriage.

The contestants compare their two experiences, and in the finale, they select whether to marry their original partner, marry someone they met on the program, or leave single.
