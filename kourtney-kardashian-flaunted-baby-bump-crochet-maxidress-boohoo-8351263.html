Kourtney Kardashian Flaunted Her Baby Bump in a Totally See-Through Crochet Maxidress

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Summer may officially be in the rearview, but that isn’t stopping Kourtney Kardashian from calling on one of the season’s biggest trends to help show off her growing baby bump. 

On Wednesday, the Poosh founder shared a roundup of OOTD photos from her collaboration with Boohoo that saw her posing in her bedroom wearing a super sexy black crochet maxidress. While all-crochet everything may have been one of summer’s most popular style hits, Kourtney made it work for fall by pairing the black frock — which featured a sheer design, flared sleeves, and a black rose-shaped chest patch — with a matching black thong and bra set. 

Kardashian accessorized only with strappy black sandals and her giant wedding ring, and she finished the look by tying up her hair in a ‘90s-style updo, save for her face-framing fringe. As for glam, she kept things bronzy and bright by swiping on a subtle contour, blushed cheeks, and a tan glossy lip.

"Are you a good witch or a bad witch?” Kourt aptly captioned her dump. “This dress from my limited @boohoo collection is made of 100% recycled polyester.”

Although the due date for Kardashian’s fourth child (and her first with husband Travis Barker) has yet to be shared, her post comes just a month after she shared an update on her pregnancy following an “urgent fetal surgery.”

“As someone who has had three really easy pregnancies in the past, I wasn’t prepared for the fear of rushing into urgent fetal surgery,” Kourtney captioned a photo of her holding Barker’s hand in the hospital. “I don’t think anyone who hasn’t been through a similar situation can begin to understand that feeling of fear. I have a whole new understanding and respect for the mamas who have had to fight for their babies while pregnant.”

She continued, “Praise be to God. Walking out of the hospital with my baby boy in my tummy and safe was the truest blessing. 🙏🏼🤍”
