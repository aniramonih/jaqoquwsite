Jevon Holland Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Jevon Holland has an estimated net worth of $1 million. This is from his career as a safety for the Miami Dolphins.

Jevon Holland signed a 4 year, $8,716,410 contract with the Miami Dolphins, including a $3,699,208 signing bonus, $6,207,809 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $2,179,103. In 2022, Holland will earn a base salary of $1,056,200, while carrying a cap hit of $1,981,002 and a dead cap value of $5,283,007.

Holland was selected in the second round by the Miami Dolphins; he was the first safety selected.

Though eligible and ranked as a #3 Canadian prospect, he was not selected in the subsequent 2021 CFL Draft.
