Is Jack Wilson Really Aboriginal?

Yes, Jack Wilson is an Aboriginal Australian.

They are the various Indigenous people of the Australian mainland and many of its islands, such as Tasmania, Groote Eylandt, Tiwi Island, Fraser Island, and many more.
