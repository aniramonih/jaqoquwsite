Park Seo-joon Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Who is Park Seo-joon?

Park Seo-joon is a South Korean actor who is active in the industry since 2011. Park Seo-joon was seen in projects like Kill Me, Heal Me (2015), She Was Pretty (2015), Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth (2016–2017), Fight for My Way (2017), and many more.

Recently he was seen in 2021’s movies Dream as Yoon Hong Dae. He is also filming for the Marvel Cinematic Universe’s film The Marvels.

This actor is active on YouTube where he has over a million subscribers. This made him the first Korean actor who received YouTube’s Gold Play Button.

Who are the parents of Park Seo-joon?

His birthdate is 16 Dec 1988 and he was born in Seoul, South Korea as Yong-Gyu Park. The name of his mother and his father’s identity is not revealed.

Further, Park’s ancestry is Asian, and has two brothers younger than him. As per his academics, he passed out from the Seoul Institute of the Arts.

Park Seo-joon- Professional Life, Career

Park Seo-joon started his official acting journey in 2011 and did many tv shows as well as films. But his first screen project was short in 2012 titled 663114.

His popular Tv series are Shut Up Family as Cha Seo Joon, I Summon You, Gold! as Park Hyun-Tae, A Witch’s Love as Yoon Dong Ha, etc. His appeared other TV projects are,

  • She Was Pretty as Ji Sung-Joon
  • Kill Me, Heal Me as Oh Ri-On
  • Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth as Sun Woo Rang / Moo Myung
  • What’s Wrong with Secretary Kim as Lee Young Joon
  • Fight for My Way as Ko Dong-man / Go Dong-nam

Park’s popular films are,

  • Concrete Utopia (2021) – Min-Sung
  • Dream (2021) – Yoon Hong-Dae
  • The Divine Fury | Saja (2019) – Yong-Hoo
  • Parasite | Gisaengchoong (2019) – Min-Hyuk
  • Perfect Game | Peopekteu Geim (2011) – Chil-Goo

As for singing, he sang songs titled “New Dreaming” in 2012, “Come into My Heart” in 2014, “Letting You Go” in 2015, and “Our Tears” in 2017.
