My Lovely Wife Chinese Drama Story

The drama story revolves around a young girl named Xu Nian. She is the only witness to her mother’s death. His father marries another woman. Xu Nian’s stepmother always bullies her and creates problems in her life. Xu Nian wants to reveal the truth behind her mother’s death. She meets the Company’s CEO Gu Ming Yan, who is also suffering in his life due to childhood traumas. Their interesting interaction led to the start of a new chapter in their lives.
