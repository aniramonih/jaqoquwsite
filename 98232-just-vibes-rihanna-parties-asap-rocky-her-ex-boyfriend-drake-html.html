Most people wouldn't want their partner to hang out with their ex but this is not the case for ASAP Rocky. The rapper was spotted partying with Rihanna and Drake in Hollywood earlier this week. Rihanna and ASAP have been dating for a few months and are still at it.

Drake was having a small get together with his friends and invited the couple to join them in enjoying the evening. According to a source, Drake has no problem with ASAP and actually approves - he's still a really close friend to Rihanna.

“Rihanna and A$AP arrived around midnight and stayed for several hours into the night,” said the source.

The group had liquor flowing throughout the night and had a very good time. Rihanna and ASAP were very close during the night and showed some public displays of affection.

Rihanna and Rocky initially started romantic rumours in 2020 after Rihanna's broke up with Saudi businessman Hassan Jameel.

Back in January, the two were seen on a dinner date at a famous Italian eatery in New York City. For the holidays, they headed out to Rihanna's home country Barbados to spend Christmas Eve with her family.
