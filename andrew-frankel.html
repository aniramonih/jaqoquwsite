Andrew Frankel is an American businessman who became the co-president of the Wall Street institutional brokerage firm named Stuart Frankel & Co. Inc. in December 1993 that was founded by his father in 1973. Aside from running his family business, he has worked as an anchor, a news reporter, an editor, as well as a camera operator. He has also been recognized for being the husband of the actress and model Bridget Moynahan.
