Coco Austin Gained Weight During Pregnancy, But Barely Find Out How Much Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Coco revealed a side by side photo of herself just before the baby’s birth on Nov. 28 and one of her now on Dec. 7, and she already has a flat tummy and tiny waist! She told her Instagram followers in the caption that,”I gained 13 pounds my whole pregnancy and now I weigh almost back to norm at 137 pounds.” That’s shocking as the baby alone weighed in at almost 6 pounds when she was born!

“All this is strange to me too, how my body took so well with pregnancy because I was mentally prepared to blow up like a whale and I was totally cool with it I really could have cared less how my body looked, it didn’t matter to me. I couldn’t wait to get a bump and rock it! I would’ve gone through anything to make a healthy baby. I had the heavens really watching over me and know everyday how blessed I am.” Pick up Ice Loves Coco season 3 here.

She claims she went on a pre-baby diet, noting “I lost 10 pounds before I even started to gain baby weight probably because I stopped drinking alcohol. And I stopped weight lifting and lost a lot of muscle.” Coco goes on to claim “I ate really healthy and took my vitamins everyday.”  Um, okay…but we’re sure most other pregnant women do the same.

This can’t sit well with other celebs who – like most normal women – put on extra weight while pregnant. That would most definitely include Kim Kardashian, 35, who admitted she put on 60 pounds and can’t understand how some stars get their post-baby bodies back so quickly. She even blogged, “I look at other celebrities and Instagram girls who are literally so skinny right after giving birth. How is that possible?!?!. “F***, I hope people don’t expect that from me, LOL!”” just one day before giving birth to son Saint West.
