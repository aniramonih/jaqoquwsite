John Lewis

Publish date: 2024-08-06

John Lewis was a more well-known political leader. He was an American politician and civil-rights activist who represented Georgia’s 5th congressional district in the United States House of Representatives from 1987 until his death in 2020. Lewis was the chairman of the Student Nonviolent Coordinating Committee from 1963 to 1966. He was also a Democratic Party leader in the United States House of Representatives.
