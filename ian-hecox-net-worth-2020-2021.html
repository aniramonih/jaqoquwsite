Early Life

Ian Hecox was born as Ian Andrew Hecox on 30th November 1987 in California, USA. His parents are Sharon Hecox and Stephan Andrew Hecox. They have both appeared in videos of the Smosh Channel. Ian also has a sister named Melissa Hecox.

Ian completed his high school graduation from Del Campo High School. This is where he met his future Smosh partner Anthony Padilla. They both were together in a science class in 6th grade.

Ian and Anthony started uploading funny videos on YouTube, which led to the birth of the now famous Smosh Channel.

Later on, Ian went to American River College but dropped out to expand the Smosh channel and also to open new YouTube channels like Smosh 2nd Channel, Smosh games, and so on.

Career

Ian Hecox and his friend Anthony Padilla started a website named Smosh.com when they were in their teens. They uploaded funny videos on the site, which was shared by their many school friends. Some of their videos found their way on the new and upcoming website YouTube.

I see the growing popularity of their videos on YouTube, Ian and Anthony started their channel Smosh on YouTube. To date, they have uploaded hundreds of videos and gained an enormous subscription base of 22 million subscribers.

Inspired by the success of their original channel, Ian and his friend launched many more channels like Smosh games, Smosh pit, Elsmosh, and Smosh France. Each channel has a regular upload schedule of various shows.

Ian has also been part of movies like Smosh: The Movie and Ghostmates (released on YouTube). Ian has given his voice in the animated movie The angry bird’s movie.

The Smosh team has also created an android app called iShut Up App and another app to access Smosh videos.

In 2017 Ian’s partner Anthony Padilla left Smosh to pursue an independent career on YouTube, leaving Ian, the sole owner of the popular channel.
