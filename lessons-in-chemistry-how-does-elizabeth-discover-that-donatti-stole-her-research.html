Lessons in Chemistry: How does Elizabeth discover that Donatti stole her research?

Publish date: 2024-08-22

In "Lessons in Chemistry" episode 4, Elizabeth Zott, played by Brie Larson, goes through a series of events that lead her to discover that Donatti had stolen her research. After being fired from her job as a lab tech at Hastings Research Institute, Elizabeth comes up with a plan to earn money and continue her experiments. She offers her services to male chemists who secretly come to her house and pay her to do their work. Through this new arrangement, Elizabeth learns that Donatti and Boryweitz were working on a DNA project using her research in Calvin's old lab.One of her clients lets it slip that Donatti had access to her research and was using it for their project. This revelation shocks Elizabeth, and she confronts Donatti in his lab with her dog, Six-Thirty, and her baby in tow. Elizabeth sees her research on the walls and accuses Donatti of stealing it. Donatti shamelessly admits to his crime and even expresses pride at having wrongfully won the Ramsen Grant. This infuriates Elizabeth and strengthens her resolve to continue the important work she and Calvin had started.To further her experiments, Elizabeth needs lab equipment and chemicals that she can't find in her kitchen. In a mischievous move, she impersonates Miss Frask, the head of Personnel at Hastings, over the phone. She convinces the suppliers that she is ordering for the Institute and has all the necessary products delivered to her home. Elizabeth transforms her house into her dream lab, allowing her to continue her research independently.These series of events not only reveal Donatti's theft but also highlight Elizabeth's determination to pursue her passion for chemistry despite the challenges she faces as a single mother. Elizabeth's relentless pursuit of truth and justice, as well as her resourcefulness in creating her home lab, showcases her resilience and intellect."Lessons in Chemistry" episode 4 also introduces other key developments in Elizabeth's life. Elizabeth forms a bond with Harriet, a fellow mother, as they navigate the challenges of motherhood and their career goals. Harriet opens up about her own struggles and aspirations, providing Elizabeth with a supportive friend during this challenging time.Additionally, the episode introduces Walter Pine, a producer at a local television station. Elizabeth confronts Walter after discovering that his daughter Amanda has been stealing lunches from Mad, Elizabeth's daughter. Despite initially turning down Walter's offer, Elizabeth is seen silently reconsidering the opportunity to host a cooking show. This sets the stage for future episodes, where Elizabeth's career takes a new turn.Overall, "Lessons in Chemistry" episode 4 not only reveals Donatti's theft of Elizabeth's research but also showcases Elizabeth's resilience, resourcefulness, and determination. The episode sets the stage for further exploration of Elizabeth's journey as she balances motherhood, her passion for chemistry, and new career opportunities.
