Tamra Judge: The Longest-Running Housewife

Tamra Judge is a well-known figure from the reality TV show Real Housewives of Orange County (RHOC). She holds the distinction of being the longest-running housewife in the history of the series, having been a part of it for several seasons. Although Tamra was an integral part of the show, her net worth may come as a surprise to many.

Tamra’s salary as a cast member of RHOC was reported to be $350,000 per season. While this is a significant sum, it does not necessarily translate into a massive net worth. Tamra’s net worth is estimated to be around $3 million, according to various sources. Though certainly substantial, it may be lower than expected for a reality star of her stature.

Besides her role on the show, Tamra has been involved in various business ventures. She had a successful career in real estate and even started her own wellness company called Vena Wellness with her husband. Additionally, Tamra and her husband owned and operated C.U.T. Fitness, a gym that gained popularity through their stint on the show, before ultimately closing it down.

Tamra Judge’s Ventures

Tamra’s foray into the wellness sector with Vena Wellness showcases her entrepreneurial spirit. The company focuses on providing CBD-based products for overall well-being. This venture not only adds to Tamra’s diverse portfolio but also aligns with her personal interests in maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

C.U.T. Fitness, which stands for “Cardio Unique Training,” was not only a business endeavor for Tamra but also an embodiment of her passion for fitness. The gym aimed to provide unique and effective workout programs to its clients. While the gym is no longer operational, it served as a platform to inspire and empower individuals in their fitness journeys.
