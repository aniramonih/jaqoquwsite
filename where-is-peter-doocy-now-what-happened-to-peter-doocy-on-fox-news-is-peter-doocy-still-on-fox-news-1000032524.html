Peter Doocy, an American journalist, currently holds the position of White House correspondent for Fox News. Doocy obtained a degree in political science from Villanova University in 2009 and embarked on his professional journey as a reporter for the Washington Examiner. In 2016, he joined the Fox News team, where he has made a significant impact.
