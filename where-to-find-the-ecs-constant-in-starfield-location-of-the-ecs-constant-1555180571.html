Where to find the ECS Constant in Starfield? Location of the ECS Constant

Publish date: 2024-08-27
In Starfield, you can find the ECS Constant by tracking the "Location of the ECS Constant" mission in your mission log and exploring various solar systems with a Grav Drive capable of traveling at least 22 light years.

Where to find the Ecs Constant in Starfield?

To locate the ECS Constant spaceship after completing the First Contact mission and choosing the first option (buying a Grav Drive), follow these simple steps:

  • Open your mission log: In the game, go to your mission log or quest journal.

  • Track the mission: Find the mission called "Location of the ECS Constant" and make sure it's active.

  • Upgrade your Grav Drive: Make sure your spaceship has a Grav Drive that can travel at least 22 light years (LY).

  • Travel through solar systems: Use your upgraded Grav Drive to move through different solar systems. The ECS Constant moves around, so you might need to jump through several solar systems.

  • Find the ECS Constant: Follow the mission markers in your log to get closer to the ship's location. The ECS Constant visits different planets on its journey.

  • Interact with the ship: Once you find the ECS Constant, you might discover new missions or quests by interacting with the ship and its crew.

    • Keep in mind that the ECS Constant's location can change as you play, so be ready for exciting adventures as you explore the game's galaxy in search of this special spacecraft.

It stands out as Bethesda's first entirely new intellectual property in nearly three decades.

    Starfield Gameplay

    Starfield is an action RPG game that lets you play from either a first-person or third-person perspective, giving you flexibility in how you experience its vast universe. This universe is massive, with over 1,000 planets, moons, and space stations scattered throughout the Milky Way galaxy. What's exciting is that this universe is a mix of procedurally generated and handcrafted content, which means you'll encounter a wide variety of environments that change based on factors like star systems, atmospheres, and how close you are to other players. 

    Article continues below advertisementArticle continues below advertisement

    One of the game's focal points is New Atlantis, a fictional mega-city where much of the action takes place. As you explore, you'll meet diverse non-playable characters (NPCs), and some of them might even join your crew to help with combat, interactions, and tasks. Players can establish outposts, forge relationships with their companions, and make choices that influence the gameplay. You'll also get to create your own character, customizing their body type, appearance, background, and traits, which offer unique skills and advantages.

    As you progress, you'll level up and unlock abilities in skill trees, allowing for different playstyles. Combat is deepened with various weapons, attachments, and even jetpacks, making battles and exploration exciting. Resource scanning informs crafting, and building outposts aids research and upgrades. Plus, you can construct, acquire, or commandeer spaceships and customize them extensively, from central components to paint jobs and armaments. Engaging in strategic spaceship combat, trading with other ships, and making important choices through dialogue are all part of the thrilling Starfield experience.
