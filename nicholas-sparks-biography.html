Who is Nicholas Sparks?

Nicholas Sparks is an American novelist and screenwriter. He is also a producer and philanthropist.

His latest novel is titled The Return and was published in Sep 2020.

Who are the parents of Nicholas Sparks?

He was born on 31 Dec 1965 in Omaha, Nebraska, US. His father is Patrick Michael Sparks and his mother is Jill Emma Marie Sparks.

His dad a future professor of business and his mom was a health worker’s assistant. Moreover, his elder brother is Michael Earl “Micah” Sparks and his late younger sister was Danielle “Dana” Sparks Lewis.

His ethnic background is a mix of German, Czech, English, and Irish. Regarding his academics, he attended the University of Notre Dame and took business finance as a major.

Nicholas Sparks- Professional Life, Career

With over 21 novels, Nicholas Sparks is one of the popular novelists. His first publication came out in 1996 and was titled The Notebook.

Then in 2003, Nicholas Sparks gave the public another second series The Wedding.

In 1998, 1999, and 2000, his other novels were published named Message in a Bottle, A Walk to Remember, and The Rescue respectively. Similarly, some of his other writing projects and their published year are,

  • A Bend in the Road (2001)
  • The Guardian (2003)
  • At First Sight (2005)
  • The Lucky One (2008)
  • The Last Song (2009)
  • The Best of Me (2011)
  • The Longest Ride (2013)
  • Two by Two (2016)
  • Every Breath (2018)

In addition, Nicholas Sparks has produced movies like The Choice, The Longest Ride, The Best of Me, and Safe Haven.
