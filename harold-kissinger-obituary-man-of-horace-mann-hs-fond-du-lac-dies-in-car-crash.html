Harold Kissinger Obituary: Horace Mann HS Fond du Lac graduate dies in Omro car crash

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Berlin, WI – The news of Harold Kissinger’s untimely passing has reverberated through social media, leaving family and friends in mourning. Tragically, Harold Kissinger passed away on Thursday, October 26, 2023, as a result of a car accident in Omro, leaving a profound sense of devastation among those who knew and cherished him. His wife’s heartfelt announcement on Facebook reflected the deep grief and profound loss experienced by his family, calling for the support of their community during this difficult time.

Sommer Joy posted on Facebook,

“My first friend. My brother. Harold Kissinger. He passed away in a car accident yesterday I don’t even have the words to say. Just broken. So I wanted to share some of my ridiculous pictures I always made him take with me and some of the good ones. You were so loved! You will be missed so incredibly much!! Life isn’t fair. Gone way too soon. Please watch over us. Give us the strength we need”
