The Hancock cast and KTLA's anchor, Cher Calvin is now engaged to Aki Oshima..

Publish date: 2024-08-26

The beautiful Filipino-American journalist and TV anchor Cher Calvin currently works in Los Angeles-based television KTLA as a news presenter.

Likewise, the six-time Emmy Award-winning Calvin is also known for her work with Studio 23’s News Central.

Early Life

Born as Cherlynn Calvin on August 1, 1974, she holds American- Philippine citizenship and belongs to Filipino ethnicity. 

Calvin was raised in New York City by former Filipino actors Roger Calvin and Georgia Holt. For her education, she studied at Marymount School of New York. 

Cher earned her BA degree in journalism and received a minor in political science from New York University.

Is Cher A Married Woman?

The 46 aged Cher is not a married woman. But, she is merrily engaged to her beloved love partner Aki Oshima. Aki and Cher first met one another in 2016.

Cher announced her engagement via Instagram on December 19, 2020. In the post, she posted a kissing picture with her fiance with the captioned "yes."

The soon-to-be-married pair is waiting for this COVID pandemic to end, to exchange the vows intimately in front of their many dearest ones. 

On August 9, 2020, Calvin shared a picture with Aki saying, 

"We’ll always have Paris. And though the pandemic has set back our wedding day from today to another time when we can celebrate with all our family and loved ones I’m still the luckiest woman to have you in my life. #postponednotcancelled."

As of now, the engaged pair is living merrily, sharing a healthy bond. They visit many exotic places together. Cher also posts romantic pictures with him on her social sites.

What Is Cher Calvin Net Worth? 

The 5-feet-6-inch tall anchor has not mentioned the exact net worth she enjoys. But, as per estimation, she has a net worth of around $1 million.

As A Reporter On Netflix's Messiah

More interestingly, this Asian journalist presented herself as a reporter in the Netflix series Messiah. One of her acting credits includes Hancock (2008) starring Will Smith and Charlize Theron.

Studio 23's former news presenter has also made her appearance in Criminal Minds (2005), Chuck (2007), and The Closer (2012).
