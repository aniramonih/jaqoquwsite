Jaz Sinclair Career

In 2013, Sinclair appeared in two episodes of HBO’s documentary series Masterclass. In one episode of NBC’s Revolution in 2014, Sinclair portrayed Kim Carson. She also appeared in three episodes of TNT’s Rizzoli & Isles as Tasha Williams.

Sinclair co-starred with Nat Wolff and Cara Delevingne in Jake Schreier’s comedy-drama Paper Towns in 2015. The movie, which was released on July 24, 2015, by 20th Century Fox, is based on the same-titled John Green novel. In 2016, Sinclair starred as Anna, a surrogate mother, in Jon Cassar’s thriller When the Bough Breaks.

The movie was released by Screen Gems on September 9, 2016. Sinclair portrayed Beatrice Bennett in The Vampire Diaries in 2017. In the mysterious horror movie Slender Man, which hit theatres on August 10, Sinclair portrayed Chloe.

Jaz played the role of Rosalind “Roz” Walker in the Netflix adaptation of The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina. She played a role in an Amazon Original spin-off of The Boys called Gen V on 11th March 11, 2021. She previously appeared as her character, a Red River Supe named Marie Moreau, in the third season of the former series.
