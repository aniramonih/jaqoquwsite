Remy Ma of LHHNY Fame Looks Amazing in Sheer Dress &amp; and Stilettos at Erica Mena's Bachelorette Part

Rapper Remy Ma set the internet abuzz after sharing stunning images of herself in a sheer black dress and stilettos at Erica Mena's bachelorette party.

Remy Ma had fans gushing over her after sharing sizzling images of herself at Erica Mena's pre-wedding party. The photos which she shared on Instagram showed the celebrity dazzling in a sheer outfit.

REMY MA'S STUNNING LOOK

In the photos, Remy struck poses in the sheer outfit, which displayed her generous curves. She complimented the look with a pair of black sandals while accessorizing with hoops, bracelets and a necklace. She wrote in her caption:

"No flash vs. flash #RemyMa (for inquiring minds: I was at @iamerica_mena lingerie themed party)."

Fans were over themselves as they lavished sweet words of admiration on the rapper. Someone gushed, "you bodied the look." Rapper Safaree was also in the comments as he showed his admiration for the reality star.

REMY MA IS A DOTING MOM

Ever since the birth of her daughter, Remy Ma has continued to be in awe of the little girl. Ma, together with her celebrity spouse, Papoose, became parents to Reminisce Mackenzie in December 2018.

Although Remy enjoys her lovely marriage and motherhood, she hasn't gone without a few dramas.

A recent snapshot shared by the doting mom showed Remy and her bundle of joy, sharing a mom-daughter moment in what seemed to be the interior of a car. Fans gushed over the duo while they agreed that the two held a striking resemblance.

A FASHIONISTA IN HER RIGHTS

It seems the femcee has a thing for sheer dresses as she rocked another equally stunning dress at the wedding ceremony of Safaree Samuels and Erica Mena.

Together with her hubby,Papoose, who looked dapper in a red and black suit, Remy Ma dazzled on a regale stairway at the event.

Beside her hubby, Remy looked pleasing in her sheer red dress while she wore her hair down in a straight and long sleek hairstyle. Although her shows were not visible, Ma's gown stole the spotlight for her.

SOMETIMES, THERE IS A HITCH

Although the "Love & Hip Hop: New York" star, Remy, enjoys her lovely marriage and motherhood, she hasn't gone without a few dramas.

Recent news show that the 39-year-old artist got involved in a lawsuit that alleged that she hit a fellow cast member, Britney Taylor, from LHHNY.

The charges have, however, been dropped as prosecutors made it known that evidence and witnesses were conflicting, which made the case difficult to prove.

Remy Ma and Papoose tied the knot in 2016 following her release after a two-year jail term for shooting a friend outside a night club.
