Jason Roy Education And Career

As for his educational background, Jason Roy attended Whitgift School after transferring from The Hawthorns School.

He was teammates with Rory Burns, a Surrey teammate and fellow England test opener, while they were both students at Whitgift School. In Surrey, Jason also participated in the neighborhood Reigate Priory Cricket Club.

He made his ODI debut in 2015 and his T20I debut in 2014. He competed for England, who won the 2019 Cricket World Cup.

As an opening batsman, Roy bats right-handed. He and Alex Hales hold the records for England’s most extensive ODI partnership (256* against Sri Lanka during their 2016 tour) and the highest ODI score in England (180 against Australia during England’s 2018 tour).

In T20I history, he was the first batter to be dismissed for blocking the field.
