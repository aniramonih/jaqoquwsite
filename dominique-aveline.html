Age, Biography and Wiki

Dominique Aveline is a French actor, assistant director, and art director. He was born on April 3, 1940 in Garches, Hauts-de-Seine, France. He is best known for his roles in films such as The Discreet Charm of the Bourgeoisie (1972), The Tall Blond Man with One Black Shoe (1972), and The Return of the Tall Blond Man with One Black Shoe (1974). Aveline began his career as an assistant director in the early 1960s, working on films such as The Longest Day (1962) and The Train (1964). He then moved into acting, appearing in films such as The Discreet Charm of the Bourgeoisie (1972) and The Tall Blond Man with One Black Shoe (1972). He also worked as an art director on films such as The Return of the Tall Blond Man with One Black Shoe (1974). Aveline has been married twice, first to actress Marie-Christine Barrault and then to actress Marie-France Pisier. He has two children, actress and singer Charlotte Gainsbourg and actor and director Lou Doillon. As of 2021, Dominique Aveline's net worth is estimated to be around $1 million.
