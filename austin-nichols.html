Austin Nichols is an American actor and director who is best known for portraying roles such as Julian Baker in One Tree Hill, John Monad in John from Cincinnati, and Spencer Monroe in The Walking Dead. He has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as The Day After Tomorrow, Wimbledon, Holiday in the Sun, The Utopian Society, The House of Usher, Masquerade, Unthinkable, Ray Donovan, Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., Walker, Deadwood, CSI: Miami, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Watching Ellie, and Parkland.

Austin Nichols studied in Casis Elementary School near Lake Austin. He completed his high school education at McCallum High School, Austin.

    Austin Nichols Facts

  • He started taking part in water skiing competitions from the age of 2. Austin was ranked number 3 in the world by the age of 13. Unfortunately, he was forced to give up the sport due to a severe shoulder injury.
  • He represented the U.S. in many junior level water skiing competitions including Pan-American Championship and Junior Olympics.
  • While studying at McCallum High School, he used to play basketball.
  • He is good friends with Jake Gyllenhaal.
  • He decided to become an actor when he was 15 years old.
  • Austin managed to acquire a manager and began his acting career after he gate-crashed a party at the Sundance Film Festival.
  • He got his big break in 2002 when he starred as Brenda Chenowith’s lover in 2 episodes of Six Feet Under.
  • For his role as Jake Hammond in the film Wimbledon, he had to learn the art of tennis and was trained by Pat Cash, a retired Australian professional tennis player.
  • He maintains a log of every movie he sees. He usually watches an average of 20 movies a week.
  • Austin was arrested in August 2007 for DUI because of a .10 blood alcohol level.
