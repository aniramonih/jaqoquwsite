The Greek basketball player Giannis Antetokounmpo is dating Mariah Riddlesprigger.

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Giannis Antetokounmpo is one of the famous names all over the sports world. Giannis is a Greek professional basketball player. Antetokounmpo is playing for the Milwaukee Bucks of the National Basketball Association (NBA) since 2013.

The sports personality also known as Greek Freak has won awards like NBA Most Valuable Player 2019 and 2020, the 2020 NBA Defensive Player of the Year, NBA All-Rookie Second Time: 2014, among others.

Dating Mariah Riddlesprigger

The 25-years-old NBA player Giannis is in a marital relationship with Mariah Riddlesprigger. Giannis' girlfriend Mariah who graduated from Rice University is also a sportsperson. Riddlesprigger is a former volleyball player who played for her high-school team.

How and when Giannis and Mariah met is still unclear. But as per his Instagram post, it seems like the duo began their romantic affair back in 2017. 

As of now, Antetokounmpo and his girlfriend are sharing a healthy bond as a perfect love partner. The duo travels many fascinating places together and has also shared romantic pictures on their respective social accounts. 

Although, Giannis and Mariah have yet not tied the wedding knot. They are the proud parents of their very first kid, a son, Liam Charles Antetokounmpo (b. February 10, 2020).

Net Worth: $60 Million

The 6-feet-11-inch (211 cm) tall Giannis who is known by his nickname Greek Freak has a net worth of $60 million which he accumulated from his successful playing career. 

As of now, Giannis plays for Milwaukee Bucks. Back in 2016, he signed a four-year $100 million contract with Milwaukee. As per Celebrity Net Worth, he earns an annual salary of $25 million. Additionally, Antetokounmpo previously played for Filathlitikos for 1 year. 

Besides, he also collected an impressive sum of wealth from his endorsement deals with various brands. Among others, Giannis's long-term endorsement deals are with the widely famous shoe brand Nike.

Early Life

Giannis was born in Athens, Greece, on December 6, 1994. His parents Veronica Antetokounmpo (mother) and Charles Antetokounmpo (father) were immigrants from Nigeria to Greece. 

Giannis has two elder brothers Francis Antetokounmpo, Thanasis Antetokounmpo (b. 1992), and two younger brothers named Kostas (b. 1997) and Alex Antetokounmpo (b. 2001).

Like Giannis, his all four siblings are the member of the NBA teams. His second older brother Thanasis also plays for Milwaukee Bucks. Similarly, Kostas plays for the Los Angeles Lakers. Likewise, their youngest brother Alexa is teamed up with UCAM Murcia of the Spanish Liga ACB.
