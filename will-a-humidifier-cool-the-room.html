Regarding this, does a humidifier lower room temperature?Humidity doesn’t change the temp. Lowering or raising the amount of evaporated moisture in the air does not have any effect on the air temp. The more moisture a warm room has the heavier the air is with warm vapors that are the same temp as the air. That’s why you run a humidifier in the winter.Additionally, can I use humidifier without air conditioner? Yes, you can use both. The humidifier will add moisture to the air in the room, where the air-conditioner will remove heat from the air. Air-conditioning removes moisture from the air like the freezer example with air blowing over your skin. They can both work in unison, but it can lower the efficiency. In respect to this, do humidifiers make it colder? Either way, they produce a cool vapor that raises a room’s humidity but not its temperature. Cool mist humidifiers may even make the room feel a little cooler.
