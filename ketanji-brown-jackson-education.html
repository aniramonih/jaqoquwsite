Ketanji Brown Jackson education background

What schools did she attend and which year did she complete?

She received her bachelor’s degree from Harvard University College(in 1992) and her law degree from Harvard Law School(in 1996).

What are Ketanji Brown Jackson’s certificates and qualifications?

She attended Harvard University with an A.B. magna cum laude. She later went to Harvard Law School, where she graduated and earned a Juris Doctor cum laude.

What is Ketanji Brown Jackson’s political party

How long has she served on the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit?

She is with the Democratic Party.

She is a jurist serving as a circuit judge on the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit. She was commissioned on June 17, 2021
