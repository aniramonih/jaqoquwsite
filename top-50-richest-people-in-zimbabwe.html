Top 50 Richest men in Zimbabwe

Strive Masiyiwa

Strive Masiyiwa is a leading telecommunication mogul. According to Strive, he was not born rich but worked hard to earn his money. His current net worth is US$2.2 billion. He is the richest in Zimbabwean and among the richest in Africa. He is the founder of Econet Wireless Zimbabwe, which is under the Econet Group headquarters in Johannesburg, South Africa, and registered in Mauritius.

Philip Chiyangwa

Philip Chiyangwa is the nephew of the late former president of Zimbabwe Robert Mugabe. He is alleged that he owns assets worth USD$2 Billion.

Shingi Mutasa

Shingi Mutasa is the owner of the tallest Shopping Mall, Joina City Centre, in Zimbabwe. Shingi is one of the richest men in Zimbabwe who is ranked the third among 50 richest men in Zimbabwe based on his assets. His Networth is not currently and officially revealed.
