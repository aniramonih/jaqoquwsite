What is Beanie Feldstein's Net Worth?

Beanie Feldstein is an American actress who has a net worth of $3 million. Beanie Feldstein was born in Los Angeles, California in June 1993. She is best known for starring as Monica Lewinsky on the 2020 television series American Crime Story. Beanie Feldstein had a recurring role on the TV series What We Do in the Shadows in 2019. She has appeared in the films Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising, The Female Brain, Lady Bird, Booksmart, and How to Build a Girl.

Beanie Feldstein has appeared in episodes of the TV series My Wife and Kids, Orange Is the New Black, and Will & Grace. She starred in the music video "Girls Like You" by Maroon 5 featuring Cardi B in 2018. Feldstein starred on Broadway as Minnie Fay in Hello, Dolly! from 2017 to 2018 with Bette Midler. Beanie Feldstein's brother is actor Jonah Hill.
