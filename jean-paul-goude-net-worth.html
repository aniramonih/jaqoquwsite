What is Jean Paul Goude's Net Worth?

Jean Paul Goude is a French graphic designer, illustrator, photographer, and advertising film director who has a net worth of $3 million. Jean Paul Goude was born in Saint-Mande, France in December 1940. Goude was an art director at Esquire Magazine in New York in the 1970s. He famous choreographed the 1989 Bicentennial Parade in Paris for the 20th anniversary of the French Revolution. In the last 30 years Goude has created well known campaigns and illustrations for brands such as Kodak, Chanel, Perrier, and Citroen.

Jean Paul studied at the Ecole Nationale Superieure des Arts Decoratifs in Paris. He worked closely with model and pop singer Grace Jones helping her with image consulting, choreography, album cover creation, and music video direction. His first television advertisement was for Lee Cooper Jeans in 1982. Goude filmed an ad for Chanel Fragrance and put model Vanessa Paradis in a birdcage. In November 2014 Goude shot the controversial cover of Paper magazine featuring Kim Kardashian.
