    Mia Gradney News Anchor

    KTRK-TV

    Mia began her career as a writer and production assistant at KTRK-TV in 1998. Her duties there included writing the morning scripts newscast, research videos, operate the teleprompter, and help in the production of The Deborah Duncan Show. Since 1998, she worked at different stations including KAVU-TV and KBMT 12-TV until 2000 as a producer, anchor, and reporter.

    Health and Fitness Sports Magazine & KIAH-TV

    In 2010, Gradney secured herself a job at Health and Fitness Sports Magazine. She worked there for two years writing monthly feature articles that covered fashion and trends. In Aug 2000, Mia began working at KIAH-TV as an anchor and reporter. Her other duties included guiding viewers through breaking news and news of the day, conducting live on-set interviews ranging in content from politics to lifestyle and pop culture, and also assisted in the engagement of social media audience.

    Her hard work was evident during her stay at KIAH-TV as she won three Emmy awards and had major achievements in her career. Among her accomplishments, she has launched an early evening newscast, led social media initiative, and created a groundbreaking billboard campaign.

    Mia Gradney KHOU-TV

    Gradney joined KHOU-TV in July 2012 where she worked as a reporter and anchor. Her duties there are to anchor the morning newscast and deliver news updates, trends, and health reports. She produces and writes health, wellness, and lifestyle features for daily life and taped segments.
