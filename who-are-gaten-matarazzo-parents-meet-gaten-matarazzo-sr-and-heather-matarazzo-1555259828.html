Who is Gaten Matarazzo?

Gaetano John Matarazzo III, born on September 8, 2002, is an American actor. He initiated his career on the Broadway stage, portraying Benjamin in "Priscilla, Queen of the Desert" (2011–12) and Gavroche in "Les Misérables" (2014–15).

Matarazzo gained widespread recognition for his portrayal of Dustin Henderson in the Netflix science-fiction-horror drama series "Stranger Things" (2016–present).

He served as the host of the Netflix show "Prank Encounters" (2019–2021). In 2022, he co-starred in the coming-of-age comedy film "Honor Society" and lent his voice to the character Boris the Dragon in the animated fantasy comedy film "My Father's Dragon."
