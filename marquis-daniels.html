Marquis Daniels is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for the Boston Celtics of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He was born on January 7, 1981 in Orlando, Florida. Daniels attended Edgewater High School in Orlando, where he was a three-time All-State selection and a McDonald's All-American. He then attended the University of Auburn, where he was a two-time All-SEC selection and a two-time All-American. Daniels was selected by the Dallas Mavericks in the second round of the 2003 NBA Draft. He has also played for the Indiana Pacers, Milwaukee Bucks, and Boston Celtics. In his NBA career, Daniels has averaged 8.2 points, 2.9 rebounds, and 2.2 assists per game. He has also been a member of the NBA All-Rookie Second Team and the NBA All-Defensive Second Team.
