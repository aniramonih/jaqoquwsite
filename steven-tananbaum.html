Steven Tananbaum Bio | Wiki

Steven Tananbaum is a famous American hedge fund manager. He is the founding partner and CEO of GoldenTree Asset Management formed in 2000. The GoldenTree has about $28 billion in assets under management (AUM) as of March 2019. He was included in the Financial Times list of “The 20 rising hedge fund stars” in 2007. In addition, he has a net worth of at least $2.28 Billion as of April 2023.

Steven Tananbaum Age

He was born on June 26, 1965, in New York City in the United States. Tananbaum is 58 years old and celebrates his birthday every year on June 26.

Steven Tananbaum Height

Steven Tananbaum Family

He was born in New York City, in the USA to devoted parents. Tananbaum holds an American nationality. His parents are David Jay and Elizabeth (Belfer) Tananbaum. His father is the president of National Retirement Programs Inc. while his mother is secretary-treasurer.

Steven Tananbaum Wife

Tananbaum married his wife Lisa A. Munster in October 1992. The couple discovered their mutual love of art while on their second date at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. The couple has made donations to art institutions. Lisa is a 1986 graduate of Northwestern University majoring in political science. The couple are the parents of Teddy Tananbaum and Tyler Tananbaum. Moreover, he resides in Palm Beach, Florida, and Westchester County, New York.

Steven Tananbaum Education

Upon graduating with his high school diploma, Tananbaum attended Vassar College. He pursued and graduated with his degree in economics in 1987 and is also a CFA charter holder.

Steven Tananbaum Goldentree

In 2000, he founded GoldenTree Asset Management where he works as the CEO. The GoldenTree has about $28 billion in assets under management (AUM) as of March 2019. The company has its headquarters in New York City and offices in Charlotte, North Carolina, Dublin, London, Singapore, Sydney, Tokyo, and also West Palm Beach. The firm specializes in credit opportunities across high-yield bonds, leveraged loans, distressed, and structured products as well as emerging markets debt.

Steven Tananbaum Billionaire

In 2000, he founded GoldenTree Asset Management where he works as the CEO. Before, he worked at MacKay Shields as an investment specialist in 1989. 1991, he became head of its high-yield group and formed the firm’s hedge fund area as lead portfolio manager in 1997.
