Publish date: 2024-08-16

Tragic Passing of British DJ and Music From Memory Co-Founder, Jamie Tiller

It is with great sorrow that we announce the untimely passing of British DJ, label owner, and promoter, Jamie Tiller, over the past weekend. The tragic accident occurred last Sunday, October 15th, leaving the music community in shock and mourning.

Jamie was a beloved figure, admired for his devotion and humility in approaching music, which brought joy to countless people. His impact on the world of music, especially the reissue and record-digging culture, is immeasurable. His legacy will continue to inspire people around the globe.

As one of the co-founders of Music From Memory, Jamie Tiller played an instrumental role in shaping the reissue landscape during the early 2010s. The label, which he established with Tako Reyenga and Abel Nagengast in 2013, is renowned for its pivotal reissues and compilations featuring artists like Gigi Masin, Napoleon Cherry, Dip In The Pool, Suzo Sáiz, and more. These releases often led to the revival of these artists’ careers. In addition, the label introduced the world to 20th-century Brazilian music, European and Japanese pop, IDM, and, more recently, new music from artists like Yu Su and Loveshadow. Jamie also managed Second Circle, a sub-label dedicated to releasing contemporary dance music.

Beyond his accomplishments as a label owner, Jamie Tiller was a highly respected and cherished DJ, known for his worldwide tours. He shared his passion for music through his performances and connected with audiences across the globe. In 2017, he contributed a memorable mix to Resident Advisor’s podcast series.
