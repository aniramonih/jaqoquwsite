Daniel Arenas Biography

Daniel Arenas is a Colombia actor who has worked in both Colombia and Mexican television well known for his role in La Gata and Los Reyes.

Daniel Arenas Career

Arenas studied acting in the United States. His television debut was in Francisco el Matemático. In 2005, he performed in the telenovela Los Reyes, playing Santiago Iriarte, a performance that gained him a nomination for the India Catalina Awards in the category of Best Actor. Three years later, he participated in the telenovela La sucursal del Cielo, playing Samuel Lizcano, a pilot of the Air Force.

His debut in Televisa was in Teresa, marking his first telenovela in Mexico. He has also worked in Amorcito Corazón, produced by Lucero Suárez, with Elizabeth Álvarez, Diego Olivera and Africa Zavala. In 2012, he made his debut in theater, with “Hercules the Musical”, where he played Hercules, the hero of Greek mythology, alongside Violeta Isfel and Miguel Pizarro. In 2013, he starred as the protagonist in the telenovela Corazón Indomable, a recreate of Marimar, produced by Nathalie Lartilleux, where he worked with Ana Brenda Contreras.

He once again worked with Nathalie Lartilleux in the 2014 telenovela La Gata with Maite Perroni, Erika Buenfil, and Laura Zapata and served as a judge in the 10th season of Nuestra Belleza Latina.
