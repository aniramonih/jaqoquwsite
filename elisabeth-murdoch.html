Elisabeth Murdoch Bio | Wiki

Elisabeth Murdoch is a famous Australian-born British and American media executive based in the United Kingdom. She worked as non-executive chairperson of Shine Group a firm she founded in 2001. The firm merged its Shine division with Apollo Global Management’s Endemol and Core Media production houses. Currently, she is now Executive Chairman of the global TV and film production and development company, Sister.

Sister had a deal with Chris Goldberg and his Winterlight Pictures company. She also produced a dating reality show called Phone Swap in 2018, which was originally aired on Snapchat. In addition, she was appointed Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE) in the 2022 Birthday Honors for services to diversity in the arts and to charity.
