Overall Meaning

In Koe Wetzel's song "Ragweed," the singer is in love with a girl who shares his affinity for the outlawed plant, Ragweed. Despite the fact that her mother and step-father disapprove of him, and the fact that she is quirky and a bit of a weirdo, the singer is smitten with her. He can't get enough of her dirty mouth and her unique personality. Despite their differences, they bond over their love for Ragweed and their shared desire to get drunk and high. The love between these two is unexpected and unconventional, but it is passionate and real.


The lyrics of "Ragweed" could be interpreted in a few different ways. Some might see it as a love story between two outcasts who find each other and connect over their shared love for an illegal substance. Others might see it as a cautionary tale about the dangers of drugs and the strange things they can make people do. Whatever interpretation one chooses, it's clear that "Ragweed" is a powerful and emotionally charged song that speaks to the hearts of many.
