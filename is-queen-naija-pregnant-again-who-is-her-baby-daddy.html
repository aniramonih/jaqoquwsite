Is Queen Naija Pregnant Again? Who Is Her Baby Daddy (Father)?Chris Sails or Clarence White?

Publish date: 2024-08-23

blurred-reality.com – Upon closer examination, we do not think Queen Naija is pregnant again. Meanwhile, the baby daddy of her first son is Chris Sails and the father of her second son is Clarence White, her current partner. Queen and Chris divorced in 2017 after she found out that he cheated on her.

Queen Naija (full name: Queen Naija Bulls) is an American singer and media personality who first gained popularity after participating in the reality show American Idol in 2014. However, it was her YouTube channel that made her a household name in the American entertainment scene.

Her breakthrough came with the release of her single, Medicine, in 2018, which received great notice and popularity across multiple media. Following that, she continued to release music, establishing a great career in R&B and soul.

Along with all the fame, Queen Naija’s personal life has regularly been a subject of concern among her fans. Recently, we have found that many people have been wanting to know if she is pregnant again. Also, some people seek to know the baby daddy of her 2 kids. Well, here is everything you need to know.

Find Out if Queen Naija Is Pregnant Again!

Queen Naija (@queennaija), who is already a proud mother of her two children (both sons), has recently been suspected of being pregnant again. The rumor of her expecting her third child is trending all across social media, especially on Instagram.

To be honest, we were a little excited when we heard about the rumor. It has already been more than 3 years since she gave birth to her second child and we thought it was the right time to have a third one. Wasting no time, we rushed to the Internet and her Instagram to find out whether or not the rumor was true.

Unfortunately, we found nothing official regarding Queen Naija’s possible pregnancy. The 28-year-old star has not given any indication about being pregnant again. We even took a closer look at her recent Instagram pictures but couldn’t find the baby bump.

Since she has not given any statement regarding the rumor and since her pictures do not show any sign of having a baby bump, we come to the conclusion that she is not pregnant.

On the other hand, there’s a chance that she might be pregnant but is hiding it from the public for now. Considering that she is only 28 years old and both of her children are sons, it would make sense that she and her partner are trying to have a daughter.

However, please note that the given information is completely based on our speculation. Nevertheless, stay tuned with us as we promise to respond to you as soon as we learn of any new information regarding her possible pregnancy.

Queen Naija Has 2 Sons: Who Is Her Baby Daddy (Father) Though?

Queen Naija and her current partner, Clarence White (@clarencenyc), frequently post pictures of their 2 children on their Instagram. They seem to be a very happy family. However, many of you might be unaware that Clarence is only the baby daddy (father) of Queen’s 2nd child, Legend Lorenzo White.

Chris Sails and Clarence White are baby daddy of Queen Naija’s first & second sons respectively.
Image Source: Instagram

Her first son, Chris Sails Jr., was actually born in 2015 when she was married to Chris Sails (@isthatchrissails). Chris and Queen split up just two years after having CJ, amid suspicions of adultery.

In the following months, Chris fathered a second baby, Blake, with Instagram personality Hehleena Million, confirming the cheating charges. However, Queen was also quick to move on. She filed for divorce from Chris and began dating her longtime boyfriend, Clarence White, a few months later.
