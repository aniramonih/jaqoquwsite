Biography

Paul Bear Bryant was born in Fordyce, AR on September 11, 1913. Iconic University of Alabama football coach for twenty-five years. He compiled a career record of 323-85-17 and led Alabama to six National Championships from 1961 to 1979. He and Joe Paterno are two of the all-time winningest coaches in NCAA football history. When he was thirteen, he agreed to wrestle a captive bear, thus earning him his nickname.
