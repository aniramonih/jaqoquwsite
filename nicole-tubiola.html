Nicole Tubiola is an American actress. Her roles include television’s Sabretooth, Wildfire, and My Fake Fiance.

She was raised in Bullhead City, Arizona. Nicole’s parents are pictured here. She is married to New Zealand-born actor Kieren Hutchison, with whom she has a son.

Nicole’s paternal grandfather was named Anthony Tubiolo (the son of Joseph Tubiolo and Josephine). Anthony was born in New York, to Italian parents. Joseph was from Lacarta, Palermo, Sicily, Italy.

Nicole’s paternal grandmother was Josephine A. Mondo (the daughter of Anthony/Antony Mondo and Alice Concetta Gugino). Anthony was the son of Italian parents, Giovanni Mondo and Crucifissa. Alice was also born in Italy.

Nicole’s maternal grandfather was James K. O. Dunn (who likely was the son of Nak/Nuk Joon Dunn and Whark/Simone Park). James was born in Hawaii, to parents from Korea. Nak was the son of Chasoo Dunn.

Nicole’s maternal grandmother was Mary Elizabeth Guischard (the daughter of Harry Julius Guischard and Dorothy Scull). Mary was born in Pennsylvania, and was of English, French, German, Welsh, and distant Swedish and Dutch, descent. Harry was born in New York, the son of John M. Guischard and Mary Evans. Dorothy was born in New Jersey, the daughter of Gideon Scull and Sallie D. Stetzer.
