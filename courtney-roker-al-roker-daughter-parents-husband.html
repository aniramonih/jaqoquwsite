Who Is Al Roker Daughter Courtney Roker Married To? Parents &amp; Husband

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Courtney Roker is widely known as the daughter of Al Roker. Unlike her parents, Courtney took her foot towards cooking rather than joining journalism. She holds the title of recipe developer at her job at Chefman.

She was adopted as an infant by Al Roker and Alice Bell in 1987 and celebrated her birthday on 14 May every year. She has recently made her way to the news of several publications through her marriage.

Who Is Courtney Roker Married To?

Courtney Roker is married to Wesley Laga on 5 June 2021 in a striking ceremony.

She took to her social media account to share a glimpse of her big day, writing, “Last night was beyond anything I could ever imagine. I celebrated my love for my husband and felt the love from everyone. My heart is filled with happiness and filled with joy.”

She added, “And I gotta say my husband’s dance moves aint to shabby. Now on to the honeymoon.”

The bride’s sister Leila served as maid of honor while brother Nick was a groomsman. She wore an Amsale gown.

Al also took to his Twitter page to celebrate the happy occasion of his eldest daughter Courtney’s marriage, saying in a post, “A perfect magical evening being there for the marriage of my first princess @Cocoroker and @djweslaga with @DebRobertsABC and #maidofhonor @LeilaRoker and #groomsman @NicholasRoker.” The family posted many pictures from the event, including pre-and-post celebration shots that illustrated how excited they were to celebrate together.

The couple announced their engagement via Instagram while quarantining in April 2020 due to the coronavirus pandemic. “Life sometimes takes you in a different direction for a reason,” Courtney wrote on Instagram. “We should have been in Paris, but with how the world is at the moment, it was placed on hold. Instead, Wes brought Paris to me with the music, lights and live cam shot of the Eiffel Tower on our TV.”

She added, “I said yeas to my best friend last night and I am over the moon! Thank you Wes for making me your partner in crime for life. I love you.” The post was shared along with some photos of the pair showcasing their love as Courtney showed her engagement ring.

Courtney’s husband, Wesley, is an actor, director, teacher, and teaching artist. As per his Instagram bio, he is also a DJ and MC for Excel Entertainment. To add to this, Wesley also has listed bartender on his Instagram.

Who Are The Parents Of Courtney Roker?

Courtney Roker is the daughter of Al Roker with his previous wife, Alice Bell.

But now, after Al’s divorce with Alice, he remarried fellow journalist Deborah Roberts in September 1995, which makes Deborah Courtney’s stepmother. Al and Deborah welcomed two children together named Leila and Nicholas, who became siblings for Courtney.

Courtney Roker with father (Al Roker), stepmother (Deborah Roberts), and two siblings (©:Instagram/Courtney Roker)

Before Father’s Day in 2019, Al revealed his true feelings about each of his kids in an interview with NBC News’ Know Your Value. Speaking about his eldest daughter, Courtney, he shared, “Courtney was not your typical kid, in that she didn’t follow the traditional path of high school in four years, then college for four years, then get a job,” he added, “That was all a bit of a struggle for her.”

In her sophomore year of college, Al explained that Courtney came to her parents and said she didn’t think higher education aligned with her path. However, she held a long-held passion for culinary and attended culinary school, and now she has made it to the world of culinary with her hard work.

As reported by AmoMama, Courtney, who is biracial, surprisingly looks so much like Al Roker. A photo was taken during 2017 on the occasion of Al’s birthday of his family and shared on Instagram. And fans couldn’t resist not find out of Courtney’s and her father's resemblance.

Due to some issues, the family was not together for about a year. And in December 2020, they finally had their family reunion because Leila recently returned home from Paris. Al’s wife, Roberts, shared the exciting news by surprising her fans over Instagram Live. “Best news ever! We wanted to share our family reconnection with all of you,” Roberts exclaimed. “Happy holidays, Merry Christmas, and we’re so thrilled. Ours just got a lot merrier.”

Al shared the clip to his Instagram, writing, “The best #christmasgift ever!!! A special #callyourmother when @cleilapatra makes a local call and reunites with @ouichefroker @nick.roker155 @debrobertsabc and me. Testing has been done. Mask wearing mandatory.”
