Is There Another Stimulus Check Coming In January 2024?

I want to let you know right away that there will be no American Rescue Plan Stimulus Checks in 2024. The USD 1.9 trillion American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 was signed into law by President Joe Biden on March 11, 2021.

The COVID-19 Stimulus Package or the American Rescue Plan are other names for this bill. The US Congress agreed to it. The move is meant to help the country get back on its feet faster after the COVID-19 pandemic hurt the economy, public health, and the current recession.

When it was first put on sale on January 14, 2021, the package built on many of the parts of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of December 2020 and the March 2020 CARES Act. What is the American Rescue Plan?

The USD 1.9-trillion American Rescue Plan aims to help Americans who are having COVID-19 financial problems and set up a fair recovery. This is a bold move that shows what Obama learned in his early years as president.

In addition to giving out USD 1,400 in direct stimulus payments, the program kept unemployment benefits the same and raised the Child Tax Credit so that it was fully refundable.

During the pandemic, it made sure that schools from kindergarten to eighth grade could safely reopen and backed COVID-19 tests and immunization programs. It also gave money to state and local governments to help them make up for lost tax money.
