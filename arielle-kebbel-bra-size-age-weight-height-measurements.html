Biography:

Arielle Kebbel was born on February 19, 1985 in Winter Park, Florida, USA. She competed for the Miss Florida Teen USA in 2002. She began her career as a model, appearing in such publications as Maxim, Men’s Health, Stuff and FHM.

After moving to Los Angeles, she successfully auditioned for her first screen role as Lindsey Lister in Gilmore Girls. In 2004, she made her film debut in the comedy plane Soul Plane.

She has since appeared in such films as Be Cool, American Pie Presents: Band Camp, Reeker, John Tucker Must Die and Aquamarine, among others.
