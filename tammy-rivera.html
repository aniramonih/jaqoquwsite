Tammy Rivera is a talented American singer, TV personality, fashion designer, stylist, and businesswoman. Tammy Rivera is also famous as the celebrity wife of rapper Waka Flocka Flame.

Early Life

Tammy Rivera was born on 30 July 1986 and she is currently 35 years old. At the time of her birth, her family lived in Tappahannock, Virginia. When she was 8 years old, her family moved to Baltimore, Maryland, following the sentencing of her father to 30 years in prison.

Talking about her family, her mother is Ramona Smith and her father is Oscar Rivera. Similarly, she has a sister named Kiesha. This personality has shared that she met her father for the first time at the age of 14 years. Likewise, her mother is African-American whereas her father is Nicaraguan. Therefore, Rivera’s ethnicity is African-American-Nicaraguan.

Career

Professionally, Tammy Rivera is a famous singer, TV personality, fashion designer, stylist, as well as a businesswoman. Rivera was previously a cast member of Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta – a VH1 reality show. As a singer, Rivera released her first single All These Kisses on 12 June 2017. Likewise, the singer released her next single titled Only One on 7 November 2017.

Moreover, on 23 April 2018, Rivera released her EP Fate. She then released her single Charlie on 10 May 2020. This single is a lead-in to Rivera’s long-awaited album Conversations. Likewise, on 22 April 2021, this singer released her single Babymama. This singer is the second to her debut album Conversations.

Additionally, this personality is famous as the celebrity wife of rapper Waka Flocka Flame. Her husband signed to 1017 Brick Squad and Warner Bros. Records in the year 2009. Following this, her husband became a mainstream artist with the release of his singles O Let’s Do It, Hard in da Paint, and No Hands. Likewise, her husband’s debut studio album is Flockaveli and it was released in 2010. Similarly, her husband’s 2nd studio album is Triple F Life: Friends, Fans & Family, and it was released in 2012.
