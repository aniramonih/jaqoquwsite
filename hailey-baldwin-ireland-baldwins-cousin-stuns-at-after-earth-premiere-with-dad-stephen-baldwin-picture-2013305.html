Hailey Baldwin Picture: Stephen Baldwin's Daughter at Movie Premiere

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Blonde and beautiful must be in the Baldwin girls' DNA. Alec Baldwin's daughter, 17-year-old Ireland, has been making waves (and headlines) as an aspiring model in recent months — and now her gorgeous cousin Hailey, also 17, is turning heads on the red carpet. On Wednesday, May 29, Stephen Baldwin's youngest daughter wowed the crowd at the Mercedes-Benz hosted New York City premiere of After Earth, starring Will and Jaden Smith.

Arriving at the Ziegfeld Theater before the movie, the blonde stunner rocked a short, fitted, black-and-white frock with sky-high nude platform heels. She was accompanied by her dad — the youngest of the four Baldwin brothers, behind Alec, Daniel, and William — who wore a black suit, blue T-shirt, and dark sunglasses.

"After Earth is an amazing movie and if you don't go see it there's something wrong with you. @officialjaden kills it!" Hailey tweeted after the premiere, giving a shout-out to Jaden Smith.

Jaden, 14, is dating Kylie Jenner, 15, who is a friend of Hailey's. Earlier in the week, Kylie and Hailey met up with a few other pals for pizza in New York's West Village.
