Early life

Throughout his life, he has had to tie the knot thrice. His first wife was Lois Bieber. Their marriage lasted between 1966 and 1974. From 1974 to 1977, he was the official husband of Jennifer Skolnick. Currently, he has a wife who is his third one, Paige Parker. The couple has two daughters.

Career

Jim Rogers got his first job in 1964 after completion of a degree at Yale University. He joined Dominick & Dominick where he first came across detailed information concerning stocks and bonds. In 1966 and the following two years, Jimi entered the United States of America Army and served as a draftee throughout the war, officially known as the Vietnam war.

In 1970, he took a different path and joined the Arnhold and S. Bleichroder investment bank. The bank gave him a chance to work together with George Soros. In 1973 both Soros and Rogers chose to leave the bank. Later that year they founded a company called Quantum Fund.

The Quantum Fund recorded great success for the next decade by its portfolio gaining over 4200%. On the other hand, the S&P grew with an increase of 47%. All these successes made the Quantum Fund among the first best international funds.

His journey in Quantum Fund ended in 1980 when Jim decided to quit the company and entirely dedicated his time to adventure. He spent most of the time riding on his motorcycle around different countries. Later, he joined Columbia Business School to work as a guest professor teaching finance.

Between 1989 and 1990, Jim worked in WCBS as a moderator. His adventure using his motorcycle for the next years were recorded in the Guinness Book of Records. In 1998, Rogers began the Rogers International Commodity Index. After that, he started visiting business shows where he gave business and investment advice.

In 2007, his whole family moved to Singapore, claiming that this place was a better environment for her daughter. For recent years, Jim Rogers has been invited to shows, programs, and forums to give business advice.
