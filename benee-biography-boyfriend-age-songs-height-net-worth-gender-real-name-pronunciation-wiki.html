Biography

Benee (born January 30, 2000), an enchanting songstress, Stella Rose Bennett, captivates the world with her musical magic. She is a talented singer and songwriter hailing from the picturesque landscapes of New Zealand. She blends pop, R&B, and electronica effortlessly to create a sonic journey.

With her melodic gems Glitter, Soaked, and the infectious anthem Supalonely, Benee casts a spell upon listeners, drawing them into irresistible rhythms and captivating lyrics. Her music transcends boundaries, resonating with the depths of the soul and sparking a sense of wonder.

Recognized for her extraordinary talent, Benee has been honoured with esteemed awards, such as the New Zealand Music Award for Single of the Year, not once but twice. Her creative journey began in 2017 with SoundCloud covers, where she honed her craft and forged her unique sound.

Since then, Benee has unleashed a series of captivating releases, each leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of her fans. Tough Guy marked her debut single, showcasing her audacity and strength. Soaked followed, enchanting audiences with its atmospheric beauty. And then came the international sensation, Supalonely, which soared to the top of charts around the world, cementing Benee‘s status as a musical force to be reckoned with.

Early Life & Education

Benee, the beautiful songbird born on the cosmic day of January 30, 2000, hails from the mystical realm of Grey Lynn, Auckland, New Zealand. Entranced by the siren call of music, she cast aside the trappings of academia, abandoning her fleeting pursuit of a communications degree at Auckland University of Technology.

Enveloped in the nurturing embrace of St Mary’s College, an all-girls sanctuary of knowledge and wisdom, Benee‘s musical voyage unfurled its wings. There, the gentle melodies of her saxophone danced through the hallowed corridors, harmonizing with the symphony of her soul.

Career

As a young prodigy, Benee‘s journey into the world of melody commenced with guitar lessons in her tender years, followed by the enchanting serenade of the saxophone during her high school days. The symphony of her life unfolded amidst the rhythmic waves of water polo, an endeavour she once believed would define her path. Yet destiny had other plans, leading her back to soul-stirring music.

Benee‘s musical spirit took flight in the sacred halls of SoundCloud, posting heartfelt covers that resonated with a growing audience. In the tapestry of her senior year, she wove her own musical tapestries, composing songs that spoke to the depths of the human experience. The universe listened intently, and she heeded its call to pursue a career in music, gracefully veering away from the path of a communications degree.

The celestial symphony of Benee‘s talent attracted the attention of Josh Fountain, a producer and member of the illustrious band Leisure. Together, they embarked on a sonic pilgrimage, birthing the mesmerizing singles Tough Guy in 2017 and Soaked in 2018. These musical offerings enraptured the senses, infusing her signature style with a touch of ethereal magic.

The harmonious vibrations of Benee‘s artistry expanded, embracing the world with the release of her debut EP, Fire on Marzz, in 2019. She unveiled glittering gems like Glitter, Soaked, and the internationally acclaimed anthem Supalonely within its sonic tapestry. These musical treasures transcended borders, gracing charts around the globe and captivating hearts with their emotive resonance.

In the creative evolution, Benee‘s grand opus, Hey U X, illuminated the world in 2020. This luminous debut album soared to celestial heights, casting a spell of enchantment upon listeners. Songs like Hey U, Nightmare, and Last Call danced upon the airwaves, intertwining vulnerability and strength in a melodic embrace.
