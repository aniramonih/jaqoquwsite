Got everything we need- Clayton Kershaw retaliates to LA Dodgers trade rumors

Publish date: 2024-08-09

With the trade deadline quickly approaching, there is much debate and speculation about big market teams, such as the Dodgers, and who will land the best available talent. Clayton Kershaw of the Los Angeles Dodgers recently said that he doesn’t believe any of the rumours about who they will sign before the August 2nd deadline.

“As far as our team is concerned, I don’t know if we have a lot of things that we necessarily need. It’s not as glaring as maybe in years past where like, ‘Hey, we really need X, we really need this,’ and they went out and got it. Regardless of what we do at the trade deadline, we’re going for it. I think that’s great. I think we know that going into every season, and spring training was no different. I mean, we signed Freddie. We got everything we need right here,” Clayton Kershaw told The Athletic.

Add in Bellinger and Trayce Thompson, whose bats have recently begun to heat up, and the Dodgers’ offence has clearly taken off. As evidenced by the team’s recent 13-0 victory over the Rockies.
