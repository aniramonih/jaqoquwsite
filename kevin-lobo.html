Kevin Lobo Bio | Wiki

Kevin Lobo ( Full Name: Kevin A. Lobo) is a popular Indian-American Chair and CEO of Stryker. He became CEO of Stryker in October 2012 and Chair of the Board in July 2014. Kevin joined Stryker in 2011 and had formerly been Group President of Orthopaedics. He serves on the Board of Directors for Parker Hannifin (NYSE: PH) and the Advanced Medical Technology Association (AdvaMed). Kevin is also a member of the Business Council.

He has a broad business career across many industries. Following holding finance positions with KPMG, Unilever, and Kraft Canada, Kevin spent eight years with Rhone-Poulenc, including leadership roles based in Europe. He later spent eight years at Johnson & Johnson, such as President of Ethicon Endo Surgery.
