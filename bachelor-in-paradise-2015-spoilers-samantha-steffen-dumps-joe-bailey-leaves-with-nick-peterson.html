Bachelor In Paradise 2015 Spoilers: Samantha Steffen and Joe Bailey Break-Up, Leaves Show With Nick

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Bachelor In Paradise 2015 spoilers tease that the episode of the reality TV show airing on August 23 will be packed full of drama – especially for Joe Bailey and Samantha Steffen. Bachelor fans have been dying to know if Samantha and Joe are getting married but according to Reality Steve’s spoilers the most-hated couple on Bachelor In Paradise actually breaks up before the show’s season finale. And, Samantha leaves paradise with another man!

Bachelor In Paradise 2015 spoilers tease that this week a new man will be arriving in paradise – Nick Peterson, a Bachelor Pad alum who originally aired on Ashley Hebert’s season of The Bachelorette. Nick has a way with the women, and apparently he has a way with Samantha as well. Spoilers tease that amidst all of the drama, Samantha Steffen promptly dumps crazy Joe Bailey and heads off on a date with Nick – at the rose ceremony that night Samantha opts to give her rose to Nick, instead of Joe.

Joe Bailey played Jeulia and then dropped her like a bad habit when Samantha arrived in paradise. And, it looks like Joe was a total douche for nothing, because Samantha wasn’t that in to him and she dumped him for Nick Peterson. Bachelor in Paradise spoilers tease that Samantha and Nick make it through the season finale and they leave paradise as an official couple. It’s unclear whether Samantha and Nick are still together, or they went their separate ways as soon as filming was over. I think it’s safe to say they won’t be getting married though, Samantha and Nick aren’t exactly the “settling down” type.
