Chip Hailstones Jail

In July 2017, Hailstone was sentenced to 15 months in prison after being charged with two counts of perjury and making false statements to police. A few years earlier, in 2011, Hailstone claimed that an Alaskan state trooper physically assaulted his daughter Tinmaiq, who was just 17 years old at the time.

In another incident, Hailstone claimed a Noorvik resident pointed a rifle at his daughter after an altercation involving his stepson. Initially, Hailstone was sentenced to three years probation but appealed the ruling.

Due to his time behind bars, Hailstone was away from his family during the very important hunting season. “I’ve got real rifles in my hand, I’m back to being my old self,” Chip said on the show following his return. “I’m going to have a good time because I missed out on this last year.”
