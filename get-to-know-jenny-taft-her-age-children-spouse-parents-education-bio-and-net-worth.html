Get to Know Jenny Taft: Her Age, Children, Spouse, Parents, Education, Bio, and Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-20

American sports broadcaster Jenny Taft is known for her work as a pundit at the Fox Network and her role as the moderator of the sports talk show Skip and Shannon: Undisputed on Fox Sports 1. She is the daughter of former NHL defensemen John Taft and Matt Gilroy. Taft also works as a reporter for the Discovery Channel’s “Battlebots.”

Profile Summary

Early Life, Family, And Education

Born in Edina, Minnesota, Jenny Taft comes from a family with a strong sporting background. Her father, John Taft, played defense in professional ice hockey for the Detroit Red Wings, and her mother, Mary Taft, was a speed skater. Her brother, Charlie Taft, also played hockey at the collegiate level. Taft excelled in sports during high school, especially in hockey, and also maintained a strong academic record. She is a fluent French speaker and pursued her education at Boston University, where she obtained a degree in broadcast journalism.

Age, Height, And Weight

Jenny Taft, at 35 years old, stands at 1.75 m tall and weighs 136 pounds. She has blonde hair and blue eyes and is of American nationality and white ethnicity, practicing Christianity from birth.

Athletic And Broadcasting Career

Athletic Career

Growing up in a family of athletes, Taft actively participated in various sports during high school, excelling particularly in hockey and soccer. She was the captain of the hockey team at Edina High School and received recognition for her contributions to high school sports. However, she decided to pursue a career in broadcasting after earning her journalism degree from Boston University.

Broadcasting Career

After graduating from college, Jenny Taft began her broadcasting career at Fox Sports North, where she covered the Women’s National Basketball Association and reported for the Minnesota Lynx. She also worked as a social media contributor for the MLB Minnesota Twins, the NHL Minnesota Wild, and the NBA Minnesota Timberwolves, as well as hosting the MLB Twins and NHL Wild games in the arena.

Marriage and Kids

Jenny Taft is married to Matt Gilroy, a former professional ice hockey player. The couple has a daughter named Gigi.

Net Worth

Jenny Taft has accumulated a net worth of $2.5 million as of 2023, deriving a substantial income from her successful career as a pundit.
